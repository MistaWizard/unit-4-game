Can you earn the perfect Pokedex Score?


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Pokemon below. By clicking on a Pokemon you will add a specific amount of Pokedex points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total Pokedex score to the random number. You lose the game if your total Pokedex score goes above the random number.

The Pokedex value of each Pokemon is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of each Pokemon will change so don't expect to breeze through every time.
